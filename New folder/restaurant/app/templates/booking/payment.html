Payment Cycle

Weekly services payment schedule: After every 7 days, invoice will be generated and payment for the same needs to be paid (in advance). Non advance payment may incur penalty of Rs. 50 /day Fortnight services payment schedule : 50 % advance must be done in case of fortnight services. Monthly services payment schedule: This will be an advance payment service, so 15 days advance payment will be required to be deposited, else, by default Weekly cycle will be applied. Our payment schedules are designed to ensure transparency and ease of payment for our customers while also ensuring that our services are financially sustainable.
